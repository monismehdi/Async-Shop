Use Case #31 – E-commerce Platform
1. Purpose

Consumers increasingly expect seamless digital shopping experiences with personalized recommendations and flexible payment options. This project will deliver a modern e-commerce platform enabling users to browse products, add items to a cart, and complete purchases efficiently. Success is measured by users easily finding and buying products, receiving relevant recommendations, and experiencing reliable, secure transactions.

2. Scope (MVP)
In Scope:

Product browsing and search
Shopping cart and checkout
Personalized product recommendations (simulated)
Multiple payment methods (credit/debit, PayPal)
Inventory and order management

Out of Scope (Explicit):

Third-party seller onboarding
Advanced AI recommendation engine
Mobile app

Notes:

Recommendation engine may use rule-based or simulated logic for MVP.
Payment gateway integration will use sandbox/test credentials.

3. Users & Personas

Persona A: Shopper. Goals: Find products, compare, purchase easily. Pain points: Slow search, confusing checkout, irrelevant recommendations.
Persona B: Admin. Goals: Monitor inventory, view orders. Constraints: No access to customer PII, cannot edit orders.

5. Functional Requirements (Behavior-Focused)

FR1. Users can register, sign in, and access their account dashboard.
FR2. Users can browse, search, and filter products by category, price, and rating.
FR3. Users can add products to a cart, view/edit cart contents, and proceed to checkout.
FR4. Users can select from multiple payment methods and complete purchases.
FR5. System displays personalized recommendations based on browsing/cart history (simulated).
FR6. Admins can view inventory levels and order status (read-only).
FR7. Users can view product reviews and ratings.
